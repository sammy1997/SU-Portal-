Manifesto points covered:

SR-C,D H.Rep , SU

Manifesto points covered:

1) Workshops of EG and Guitar were conducted in SR. We have conducted 5 sessions (2.5 hrs each) of guitar workshop with the help of student’s in music club.
2) Pump is provided in cycle parking of A and B block. Funds were generated through raddhi from students to spend on hostel needs like cycle pump.
3) Intra Hostel FIFA, Counter Strike and other various gaming competitions in collaboration with sports and cultural sec.
4) Hot water and milk is available in redi. Redi time was increased during exams.
5) Frequency of fumigation was increased as per seasonal needs.
6) Quilts were provided to inmates during winter in last semester.
7) An online survey regarding hostel condition, working of representatives, and feasible suggestions for the betterment of hostel. We shared survey summary and work progress with Student Union. 8) Around 100 people participated in the cricket tournament and the table tennis tournament was also well received with a participation of around 40 participants.
9) A Google Drive was created and the solutions to the continuous evaluation problems of Engineering Graphics were uploaded. Moderators check the solutions and approve them. This drive with credible solutions is shared with students from all sections.
10) Coffee Machine was installed in Common Room to facilitate students during examinations. The service was also further extended to benefit students daily.
11) A Series of extra classes to be engaged by Dr. Manoj Kannan and his team is being arranged to address student’s difficulties.
12) Tutorial question papers from many tutorial sections are collected and shared on google drive to ensure that the students can practice various kind of questions before the compre’s. Evaluative component questions in EG are uploaded as soon as the evaluation is over so that the students can practics questions of that topic to before there day of evaluation. Similarly, in some other subjects like Thermodynamics tutorial questions are uploaded as soon as the test is over just to give glimpses about the type and difficulty level of the question in the tutorial question paper
